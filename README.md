# bizzaOS - Your Personal Cloud Frontend

bizzaOS is a lightweight web-based frontend designed to manage your homelab from a single, clean interface.  
It focuses on simplicity, control, and flexibility while staying minimal and developer-friendly.

---

## Why do you need bizzaOS?

Managing a homelab often involves juggling multiple services, ports, dashboards, and configs.

bizzaOS aims to:
- Provide a unified interface for your services
- Reduce friction in managing self-hosted apps
- Keep everything accessible in one place
- Stay lightweight and customizable
- Cater to powerusers and beginers alike
